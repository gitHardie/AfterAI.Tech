# Google launches AI music generator Lyria 3 Pro, says it was trained on data it has the right to use

<p>        Google is making AI music longer and more widely available: Lyria 3 Pro generates songs up to three minutes long, complete with verses, choruses, and bridges. Google says the model was trained on content it has the right to use. That sets it apart from rival music generator Suno, which is facing legal battles with record labels over potential copyright infringement.</p>
