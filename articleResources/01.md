# Norway bans generative AI tools in elementary schools to protect kids' basic learning skills

<p>        Norway is banning generative AI tools in elementary schools starting in late August. Students in grades 1 through 7 won't be allowed to use AI at all; secondary schools will permit it only under supervision. Prime Minister Stoere says children must first "learn to read, write, and do math."</p>
