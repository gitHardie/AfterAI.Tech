<p>        Google releases Gemini 3.5 Live Translate, an audio model for real-time translation across more than 70 languages. The system translates continuously without waiting for a sentence to end and claims to preserve the speaker's tone, pace, and pitch. In Google Meet, language support jumps from five to over 70 languages.</p>
