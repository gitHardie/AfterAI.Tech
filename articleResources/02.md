<p>        A reasoning model from OpenAI has disproved a conjecture by mathematician Paul Erdős on unit-distance geometry that stood open since 1946 - using tools from algebraic number theory that experts never expected in this context. Fields Medalist Tim Gowers calls the result "a milestone in AI mathematics" and warns: "We have still probably entered an era where it will become very difficult for humans to compete with AI at solving mathematical problems."</p>
